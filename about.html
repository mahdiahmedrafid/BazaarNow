Our Story

BazarNow was born from a simple frustration: the difficulty of getting fresh, quality groceries delivered reliably in Bangladesh's bustling cities. Founded in 2024, we started with a small team in Dhaka with one goal - to make bazar shopping as easy as a few taps on your phone. Today, we serve thousands of households across major cities, partnering with 200+ local farms and suppliers, while championing sustainability through our Green Choice initiative.
